About Us

Who We Are

Christ Divine Communication Nig LTD is a leading multi-sector business organization based in Awka, Anambra State, Nigeria. Since our establishment, we have been committed to delivering excellence across three major divisions: Office Equipment & ICT Solutions, Professional Training, and Construction Materials Supply.

Our journey began with a vision to provide comprehensive business solutions that meet the evolving needs of our clients. Today, we proudly serve hundreds of satisfied customers across Nigeria, offering quality products, professional services, and industry-leading training programs.

With three specialized divisions working in synergy, we bring together expertise in ICT, education, and construction to create a unique value proposition for businesses and individuals alike.
